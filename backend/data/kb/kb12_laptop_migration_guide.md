# Data Migration and Backup Guide for Workstations

## Problem
Users replacing their laptops need to back up and transfer files, browser profiles, and application settings to their new device.

## Resolution
*   **Desktop & Documents Backup**:
    - Ensure your corporate **OneDrive** client is active. Look for the blue cloud icon in your taskbar.
    - Right-click the OneDrive icon > Settings > Backup > **Manage backup**.
    - Ensure Desktop, Documents, and Pictures folders are toggle-active to sync to the cloud.
*   **Browser Profiles**:
    - Open Edge or Chrome. Log in using your company credentials and toggle **Sync Profile** on.
*   **Local Data**:
    - Any unsynced data under `C:\LocalStore` must be copied to a secure network drive or external hard drive before decommissioning.
