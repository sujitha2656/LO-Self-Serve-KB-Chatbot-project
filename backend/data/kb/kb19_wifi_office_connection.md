# Connecting to Office Wi-Fi Networks

## Problem
Employees visiting physical offices are unable to access the corporate intranet or experience slow connections on guest Wi-Fi.

## Resolution
1. Search your available network list for **Corporate-Wi-Fi**.
2. Click **Connect**.
3. When prompted, enter your corporate email address and current password.
4. Accept the server security certificate (`radius.company.com`) if prompted.
5. Do not use **Guest-Wi-Fi** for work computers. Guest Wi-Fi blocks corporate intranet portals, printing, and file shares.
