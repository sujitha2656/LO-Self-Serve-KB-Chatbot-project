# Home Wi-Fi Connectivity and Performance

## Problem
Remote workers experience dropouts or slow network response while connecting from their home network.

## Resolution
1. Open a browser and run a speed test: `https://www.speedtest.net`. You need at least 15 Mbps download/upload for stable Teams calls.
2. If speed is low:
   - Move closer to your Wi-Fi access point.
   - Disconnect unnecessary personal devices (e.g. streaming TVs, gaming consoles) during work hours.
3. Turn off your corporate VPN during high-bandwidth operations if not accessing corporate files.
4. Try using a wired Ethernet connection to bypass local wireless channel interference.
