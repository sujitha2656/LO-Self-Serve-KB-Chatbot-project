# password_reset

## Standard Password Reset Procedure
This guide describes how users can reset their passwords autonomously.

1.  Open the company login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link.
2.  Input your official email address.
3.  You will receive a password reset link via email within 5 minutes.
4.  Click the link and input a new password that complies with our password policy.

## Password Requirements
*   Minimum 12 characters.
*   Must contain at least 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter, 1 number, and 1 special symbol.
*   Cannot reuse any of your last 5 passwords.
*   Reset links expire after 1 hour.
