# Microsoft Teams Audio and Video Troubleshooting

## Problem
Users cannot share their screen, display their webcam feeds, or hear colleagues during Microsoft Teams online calls.

## Resolution
*   **Camera or Microphone Not Working**:
    - In Teams, click your profile icon (top right) > **Settings** > **Devices**.
    - Under Audio devices, ensure the correct headset or speaker is selected.
    - Click **Make a test call** to verify recording.
*   **Permissions Issue (macOS)**:
    - Open System Settings > Privacy & Security.
    - Check **Camera**, **Microphone**, and **Screen Recording** to ensure Microsoft Teams toggle is active. Restart Teams to apply permissions.
