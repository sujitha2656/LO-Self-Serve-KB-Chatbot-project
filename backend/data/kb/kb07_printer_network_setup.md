# Connecting to Office Network Printers

## Problem
Employees working in physical corporate offices need to add and print to network printers.

## Resolution
1. Ensure you are in the office and connected to the corporate Wi-Fi or wired network. VPN must be turned off.
2. **On Windows**:
   - Open Settings > Devices > Printers & Scanners.
   - Click **Add a printer or scanner**.
   - Wait for discovery. Select the office printer matching your location code (e.g., `PRN-HQ-FL02-COL`) and click **Add device**.
3. **On macOS**:
   - Open System Settings > Printers & Scanners.
   - Click the **Add Printer, Scanner, or Fax...** (+) button.
   - Select the printer from the list, ensure the "Secure AirPrint" driver is selected, and click **Add**.
