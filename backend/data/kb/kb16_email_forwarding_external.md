# Policy on External Email Forwarding Block

## Problem
Emails forwarded automatically to personal email accounts (e.g. @gmail.com) fail to deliver, returning a bounce-back error.

## Resolution
*   **The Policy**:
    - Auto-forwarding from company mailboxes to external domains is blocked by corporate security rules to prevent data leaks.
*   **Workaround**:
    - If you are traveling, use the **Outlook Web App** (`https://outlook.office.com`) to check your email on a secure personal device.
    - For notifications, configure the Outlook Mobile App on your work or personal phone.
