# Printer Hardware and Offline Troubleshooting

## Problem
Printers display "Offline" status, paper jams, or quality problems, blocking document execution.

## Resolution
*   **Fixing Offline Status**:
    - Disconnect corporate VPN client if you are printing from within the physical office.
    - Reboot the printer. Unplug the ethernet cable, wait 30 seconds, plug it in, and verify the network indicator turns solid green.
*   **Clearing Jams**:
    - Locate the flashing amber light indicating the jam location (Drawer A, B, or Duplexer).
    - Open the side drawer door, pull out the tray, and pull the sheet out in the direction of the rollers to avoid tearing.
    - Close the trays firmly.
