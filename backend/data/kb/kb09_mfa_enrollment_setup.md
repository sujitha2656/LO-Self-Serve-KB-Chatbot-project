# Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Enrollment

## Problem
New employees need to configure Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) to access corporate cloud platforms.

## Resolution
1. On your computer, open a browser and go to: `https://aka.ms/mfasetup`.
2. Log in using your company email and temporary setup password.
3. The screen will say "More information required". Click **Next**.
4. Download the **Microsoft Authenticator** app on your iOS or Android mobile device.
5. In the app on your phone, click **Add Account** > **Work or school account** > **Scan QR Code**.
6. Scan the QR code displayed on your computer screen.
7. Click **Next** on your computer. A test approval notification will be sent to your phone. Approve the sign-in.
8. Store the provided backup phone verification number in case you lose access.
