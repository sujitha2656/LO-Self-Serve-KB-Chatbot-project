# Laptop Replacement and Refresh Requests

## Problem
Workstations that are over 3 years old, damaged, or slow need replacement through our standard hardware lifecycle.

## Resolution
1. Navigate to the employee intranet portal: `https://intranet.company.com/hardware`.
2. Check your hardware eligibility status. Laptops are refreshed every 36 months of lease.
3. If eligible, select your model preference (Standard ThinkPad or Macbook Pro) and click **Request Hardware Refresh**.
4. Fill out your department billing code, manager approval email, and shipping address.
5. Once your manager approves the ticket request, your laptop order will ship within 5 business days.
6. Return your old workstation within 14 days of receiving your new device.
