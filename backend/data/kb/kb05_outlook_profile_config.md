# Outlook Profile Setup and Configuration

## Problem
New employees or users with corrupted mail profiles need to set up their corporate email accounts on Microsoft Outlook for desktop.

## Resolution
1. Open the Control Panel, search for **Mail**, and open **Mail (Microsoft Outlook)**.
2. Click on **Show Profiles...** and then click **Add...**.
3. Name the profile (e.g., "Corporate Mail") and click **OK**.
4. In the Add Account window, enter your name, email address (e.g., `user@company.com`), and password.
5. Click **Next** to let Outlook auto-discover your Office 365 mailbox settings.
6. A Microsoft login prompt will appear. Enter your credentials and complete the 2FA prompt on your phone.
7. Click **Finish**. Ensure this new profile is selected under "Always use this profile", then open Outlook.
