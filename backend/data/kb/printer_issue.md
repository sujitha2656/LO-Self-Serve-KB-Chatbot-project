# printer_issue

## Local Office Printer Troubleshooting
This article guides you through resolving connection issues with the office printers.

## Connect to Office Printers (Network-based)
1.  Ensure you are connected to the office Wi-Fi network (Corporate-Wi-Fi) or plugged into an office ethernet port. (You cannot connect via the guest Wi-Fi).
2.  On Windows:
    *   Go to Settings > Devices > Printers & Scanners.
    *   Click "Add a printer or scanner".
    *   Select the office printer (e.g., `PRN-HQ-FLOOR2`) from the list.
3.  On macOS:
    *   Go to System Settings > Printers & Scanners.
    *   Click the "+" button.
    *   Select the office printer and click "Add".

## Common Troubleshooting
*   **Offline Status**: Restart the printer, wait 1 minute, and verify it is connected to the network. Check if your computer has VPN active (disconnect VPN while printing in the physical office).
*   **Paper Jam**: Open the main side drawer (indicated by the flashing orange light) and gently pull out any jammed paper sheet. Close the drawer firmly.
