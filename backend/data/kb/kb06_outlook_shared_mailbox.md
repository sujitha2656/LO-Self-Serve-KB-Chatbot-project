# Adding a Shared Mailbox in Outlook

## Problem
Team members who have been granted access to a shared inbox (e.g., `info@company.com`) need it to display in their Outlook client.

## Resolution
*   **Automatic Ingestion (Standard)**:
    - Shared mailboxes auto-map to your folder structure within 24 hours of permissions assignment. Restart Outlook to force a check.
*   **Manual Ingestion (Alternative)**:
    1. Open Outlook and click **File** > **Account Settings** > **Account Settings**.
    2. Select your email and click **Change**.
    3. Click **More Settings** > **Advanced** tab.
    4. Click **Add...** under the Mailboxes section.
    5. Enter the shared email address and click **OK** > **Apply** > **OK**.
    6. The folder will appear in your left sidebar.
