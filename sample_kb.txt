Employee Knowledge Base

Password Reset

Employees can reset their password by clicking the "Forgot Password" option on the login page. A password reset link will be sent to the registered email address.

VPN Access

Employees requiring remote access must submit a VPN access request through the IT Service Portal. Manager approval may be required before access is granted.

Leave Policy

Employees can apply for leave using the Leave Management System. Leave requests must be submitted in advance and approved by the reporting manager.

Employee ID Card

New employees will receive an ID card after onboarding. Lost or damaged ID cards can be replaced by submitting a request through the HR Portal.

Timesheet Submission

Employees must submit their weekly timesheets through the Timesheet Portal before the end of each week. Late submissions may require manager approval.
